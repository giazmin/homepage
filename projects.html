Projects

I have been involved in 4 projects so far.

The first one was a "The College Sleep Project" at MIT Media Lab, in which we studied selected groups of MIT students for sleep patterns with wearables. I mostly did data analysis and mobile app design to study the sleep patterns. I was a part of Affective Computing, which joined with the Harvard Medical School and Brigham Women's Hospital for the project.

The second project was in Korea at Korea Institue of Science and Technology. I designed and implemented a web platform for sketching and rendering chmical molecules. I also worked with the 3D printer to print the molecular structures, which was later used to experiment its reaction to acoustic waves.

The third was a project at Sloan School of Management, titled "Big Data, Predictive Analytics, and Decision Making". I was in the data analysis team and thus contributed in collecting and analyzing high volume data from police stations to predict routes of dispatch.

The final project was a done this summer on Music technology. The purpose of the project was to use machine learning to extract emotions from pieces of music(MIDI format). I designed the features that could represent emotions and implemented feature extraction with JAVA.
